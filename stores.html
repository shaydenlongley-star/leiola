How It Works

Pre-Order & Collect at the Airport

Save time and money by pre-ordering your duty-free before you travel. It's easy — and you'll save 10% when you order online.

1

Browse & Order

Browse our product range online, by phone (+676 25 222) or by email at sales@leiola.to and place your order.

2

Pay at Our Store

Visit any Leiola store to pay for your order. Use code DISCOUNT online for 10% off (excludes deals and jerseys).

3

Collect at the Airport

Present your order ID at our Fua'amotu arrival or departure shop and collect your duty-free goods — packed and ready.
