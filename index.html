Who We Are

Tonga's Sole Duty-Free Distributor

Leiola Group Ltd has been Tonga's premier duty-free retailer since 1994 — the sole distributor of duty-free goods across the Kingdom. We specialise in world-class brands at the best prices in the Pacific, from premium spirits and fine wines to renowned fragrances and official rugby jerseys.

A fully-owned subsidiary of Pacific Retail Ltd, Leiola operates six locations across Tonga including arrival and departure shops at Fua'amotu International Airport, ensuring you can shop duty-free whether you're coming or going.
