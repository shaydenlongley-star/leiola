KooGa Sportswear

Leiola Group is the exclusive agent and distributor of KooGa Sportswear in the Kingdom of Tonga. KooGa is a premium rugby and sports apparel brand recognised across the Pacific — worn by serious players and fans alike.

As the sole KooGa distributor in Tonga, Leiola carries the full range of KooGa rugby kits, training wear and accessories. Whether you're a player or a supporter, if you want KooGa in Tonga, Leiola is your only source.

Official Distributor · Tonga Brand 02

Heineken Beer

Leiola Group is the official Heineken distributor for the Kingdom of Tonga. Heineken — the world's most recognised premium lager — is available cold and fresh at our store locations across the Kingdom.

Brewed to the same original Dutch recipe since 1873, Heineken's crisp, refreshing taste has made it a global icon. In Tonga, there's only one place to get the genuine article: Leiola Group.

Available by the bottle at our Nuku'alofa and Vava'u stores. Bulk and wholesale enquiries welcome.

Official Jerseys
Official Agent · National Teams Brand 03

'Ikale Tahi & Mate Ma'a Tonga

Leiola is the official agent for the authentic jerseys of Tonga's national rugby union team ('Ikale Tahi) and national rugby league team (Mate Ma'a Tonga). These are the real, licensed jerseys — not replicas.

Available in men's, women's and junior sizes, these jerseys are a proud symbol of Tongan identity and sporting passion. Whether you're watching from the stands or the couch, wearing the official jersey means everything.
