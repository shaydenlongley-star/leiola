Know Your Limits

Duty-Free Allowances

Make sure you stay within your country's duty-free allowance when bringing goods home. Here's a quick guide.

Tonga Inbound Allowances

Per person arriving into Tonga

Alcohol: 2.25 litres of spirits OR 4.5 litres of wine OR 4.5 litres of beer

Tobacco: 500 cigarettes (2 × cartons of 250 sticks) OR 500 grams of tobacco

Age restrictions: 18+ for alcohol · 16+ for tobacco. Full customs charges apply on goods in excess of allowances.
