Travel Smart

Know Your Duty-Free
Allowances

Before you shop, know what you can bring home. Here are the duty-free limits for Tonga and destinations around the world.

Arriving Into Tonga

Tonga Inbound Allowances

Per person entering the Kingdom of Tonga. Age restrictions apply: 18+ for alcohol, 16+ for tobacco.

2.25L Spirits
4.5L Wine or Beer
500 Cigarettes

Tonga Customs — Detailed Breakdown

Spirits (over 22% ABV) 2.25 litres — approximately 3 × 750ml bottles
Wine or Beer 4.5 litres — approximately 6 × 750ml bottles of wine, or a case of beer
Cigarettes 500 cigarettes (2 × cartons of 250 sticks), OR 500 grams of tobacco
Age Restrictions 18+ for alcohol  ·  16+ for tobacco. Full customs charges apply on goods in excess of allowances.
