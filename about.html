Our History

Three Decades of Duty-Free Excellence

Leiola Group Ltd was formed in 1994 as a fully-owned subsidiary of Pacific Retail Ltd, which operates several major businesses across Tonga. Since our founding, we have grown to become the Kingdom's only duty-free retailer — holding the sole duty-free distribution licence in Tonga.

Specialising in tax and duty-free discount shopping, we feature famous name brands from across the globe. Our commitment has always been the same: offering quality duty-free and retail shopping in Tonga with an ever-growing range of products at very affordable prices.

As the sole distributor of duty free in Tonga, we are committed to improving our customer relations, providing excellent service and maintaining competitive prices across the Pacific.

What We Offer

The Best of the World, Duty Free

We offer the largest selection of wines and liquors from top producers, a comprehensive range of renowned fragrances, tobacco at duty-free prices, confectionery from the world's favourite brands, and exclusive sportswear and rugby jerseys.

Looking for a special gift? We carry Worth & Douglas gold jewellery — necklaces, anklets and more — as well as a great collection of handbags, scarves, men's belts, cufflinks, and locally crafted handmade gifts.

Come shop with us at Leiola and enjoy a relaxed, easy shopping experience with friendly staff who know their products inside and out.
